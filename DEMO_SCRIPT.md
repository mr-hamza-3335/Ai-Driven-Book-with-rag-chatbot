# 90-Second Demo Script

## AI-Driven Textbook with Embedded RAG Chatbot

---

### Opening (0-10 seconds)
"I built an AI-driven textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics with an embedded RAG chatbot."

### Show Book Structure (10-25 seconds)
*Navigate through sidebar*
"The book has 6 comprehensive chapters covering:
- Introduction to Physical AI
- ROS 2 fundamentals
- Gazebo simulation
- NVIDIA Isaac
- Vision-Language-Action models
- A capstone project"

### Demo Book-Wide RAG (25-45 seconds)
*Click chat button, type question*
"The chatbot answers questions about the entire book. Let me ask: 'What is Physical AI?'"

*Show response with citations*
"It retrieves relevant content from the book and cites the source chapter."

### Demo Selected Text Mode (45-65 seconds)
*Highlight text on page*
"What makes this unique is Selected Text Mode. I highlight any text..."

*Type question in chat*
"...and ask 'Explain this concept.' The chatbot answers based only on my selection."

### Show Zero-Hallucination (65-80 seconds)
*Ask off-topic question*
"Watch what happens with an off-topic question: 'What is the capital of France?'"

*Show response*
"It refuses to hallucinate - answers only come from the book content."

### Tech Stack Close (80-90 seconds)
"Built with Docusaurus, FastAPI, Gemini API, and Qdrant. All open source. Thank you!"

---

## Key Points to Emphasize

1. **6 chapters** of original content
2. **Embedded chatbot** on every page
3. **Selected text mode** for contextual Q&A
4. **Zero hallucination** - only answers from book
5. **Source citations** with every response
6. **All Gemini-powered** - embeddings and LLM
